Welcome to Madman T-Shirts LLC!

We're your go-to destination for custom screen-printed apparel right here in the heart of Chippewa Falls. We take immense pride in being a local, family-oriented business, and it's our pleasure to serve our vibrant community.

Our mission is to provide you with high-quality, personalized apparel that reflects your unique style and the spirit of our tight-knit town. At Madman T-Shirts, we believe in creating more than just clothing; we create connections and memories.

Whether you're looking to showcase your local pride or commemorate a special family event, we've got you covered. From custom-designed t-shirts for your family reunion to Chippewa Falls-themed hoodies, we cater to all your needs.

Every design is crafted with love and attention, ensuring that your apparel is as special as the moments it represents.

The best way to reach out to us is by sending an email to info@madmantshirts.com. We're excited to collaborate with you and turn your ideas into wearable art.
